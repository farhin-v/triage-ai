DAMAGED GOODS POLICY

Effective Date: January 2024

1. REPORTING DAMAGED GOODS
Customers must report damaged goods within 48 hours of receiving their order. Reports made after 48 hours may not be eligible for a replacement or refund. To report damaged goods the customer must provide their order ID, a description of the damage, and photos of the damaged item and packaging.

2. ASSESSING DAMAGE CLAIMS
When a damage claim is received the support team must review the photos and description provided by the customer. If the damage is clearly visible and the claim is made within 48 hours the claim must be approved immediately. If the damage is not clearly visible from the photos the support team must request additional information from the customer.

3. REPLACEMENT POLICY
Customers with approved damage claims are entitled to a free replacement of the damaged item. The replacement is dispatched within 2 business days of claim approval. The customer does not need to return the damaged item unless specifically requested by the support team.

4. REFUND POLICY FOR DAMAGED GOODS
If a replacement is not available the customer is entitled to a full refund including the original shipping cost. The refund is processed within 3 to 5 business days of claim approval. Customers cannot receive both a replacement and a refund for the same damaged item.

5. ESCALATION OF DAMAGE CLAIMS
Damage claims must be escalated to the shipping team immediately in the following situations. The customer reports that multiple items in their order were damaged. The customer reports that the packaging was clearly mishandled by the courier. The customer expresses frustration or threatens legal action. All escalated damage claims must be resolved within 2 business days.
