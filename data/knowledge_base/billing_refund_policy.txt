BILLING REFUND POLICY

Effective Date: January 2024

1. REFUND ELIGIBILITY

Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 calendar days of purchase if any of the following conditions are met:

- The service was not activated after successful payment.
- The service was not delivered due to a verified technical issue on the company side.
- The customer was charged incorrectly for a higher plan than purchased.

Refund requests made after 30 days are not automatically eligible and will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis by the Billing Team.

Refund approval after 30 days is only granted in exceptional cases such as verified system errors or duplicate billing.

2. DUPLICATE CHARGES

If a customer is charged more than once for the same transaction:

- The duplicate charge will be refunded in full.
- The refund is processed within 3 to 5 business days after verification.
- Customers must provide at least one of the following:
  - Transaction ID
  - Payment receipt
  - Bank statement showing duplicate charge

3. FAILED PAYMENT REFUNDS

If a customer’s payment was successfully deducted but the service was not activated:

- The issue must be verified by the Billing Team.
- If confirmed, a full refund is issued within 5 to 7 business days.
- Alternatively, the service may be activated immediately if the customer still wants to continue without refund.

If the payment was only authorized (not captured), no refund is required. The pending hold will be released according to the customer’s bank policies.

4. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

Customers may request a refund by contacting Support and providing:

- Order ID or Transaction ID.
- Registered email address.
- Reason for refund request.

Refund requests are reviewed within 2 business days of submission.

Approved refunds are processed based on the timelines defined in Sections 2 and 3.

5. NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS

The following are non-refundable:

- Setup fees and one-time configuration charges.
- Partially used subscription periods (unless service was unavailable for more than 48 consecutive hours).
- Add-on services or promotional credits once consumed.

Exception:
If the service is unavailable for more than 48 consecutive hours due to a verified system outage, the customer may be eligible for a prorated refund or account credit.
