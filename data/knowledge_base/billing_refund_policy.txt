BILLING REFUND POLICY

Effective Date: January 2024

1. REFUND ELIGIBILITY
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase if the service was not delivered as described. Refund requests made after 30 days will be reviewed on a case by case basis.

2. DUPLICATE CHARGES
If a customer has been charged more than once for the same transaction, the duplicate charge will be refunded within 3 to 5 business days. Customers should provide their transaction ID and bank statement as proof.

3. FAILED PAYMENT REFUNDS
If a payment was deducted from a customer's account but the service was not activated, the full amount will be refunded within 5 to 7 business days.

4. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND
Customers can request a refund by contacting support with their order ID and reason for the refund. All refund requests are processed within 2 business days of approval.

5. NON REFUNDABLE ITEMS
Setup fees and one time configuration charges are non refundable. Partially used subscription months are non refundable unless the service was unavailable for more than 48 hours.
