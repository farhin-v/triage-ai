GENERAL FEEDBACK POLICY

Effective Date: January 2024

1. HOW TO SUBMIT FEEDBACK
Customers can submit feedback through the feedback form on the website, by replying to any support email, or by contacting support directly. All feedback is reviewed by the customer experience team within 5 business days. Customers who submit feedback receive an acknowledgement email within 24 hours confirming their feedback has been received.

2. TYPES OF FEEDBACK
Positive feedback is forwarded to the relevant team and used for performance recognition. Negative feedback is treated as a priority and reviewed by the customer experience manager within 2 business days. Suggestions for product improvements are forwarded to the product team and added to the feedback backlog for review during quarterly planning.

3. ACTING ON FEEDBACK
The customer experience team reviews all feedback monthly and identifies patterns and recurring issues. Recurring negative feedback on the same topic is escalated to the relevant department head for immediate action. Customers whose feedback leads to a product or process improvement are notified by email.

4. FEEDBACK RELATED COMPLAINTS
If a customer submits feedback expressing serious dissatisfaction with the service the support team must treat it as a complaint. Complaints must be acknowledged within 24 hours. The customer experience manager must review all complaints within 2 business days. A resolution must be provided within 5 business days.

5. ANONYMOUS FEEDBACK
Customers can submit anonymous feedback through the feedback form by leaving the contact details fields blank. Anonymous feedback is reviewed and recorded but cannot be followed up with the customer. The support team must never attempt to identify the source of anonymous feedback.
