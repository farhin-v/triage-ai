ACCOUNT CREATION POLICY

Effective Date: January 2024

1. CREATING A NEW ACCOUNT

Customers may create a new account by visiting the signup page and providing:
- Full name.
- Email address.
- Password.

Passwords must contain:
- At least 8 characters.
- At least 1 uppercase letter.
- At least 1 lowercase letter.
- At least 1 number.

A verification email is sent immediately after signup.

Customers must verify their email address within 24 hours. If the email is not verified within 24 hours, the account will be automatically deactivated and login access will be blocked until verification is completed.

2. ACCOUNT VERIFICATION

If a customer does not receive the verification email, they should:
- Check their spam or junk folder.
- Confirm that the email address entered during signup is correct.

If the verification email is not received, the Support Team may manually resend the verification email.

A maximum of 3 verification emails may be sent to the same account within a 24-hour period.

If the verification link expires, the customer may request a new verification email.

3. DUPLICATE ACCOUNTS

Customers cannot create multiple accounts using the same email address.

If a customer attempts to register with an email address that already exists in the system:
- The customer will be notified that the email is already registered.
- The customer will be directed to the Login page or Forgot Password process.

The Support Team must not create duplicate accounts for an email address that already exists in the system.

4. BUSINESS ACCOUNTS

Customers requesting a Business Account must provide:
- Company name.
- Company registration number.
- Business email address.

Business Account verification typically takes between 3 and 5 business days.

During the verification period:
- Limited account access may be provided.
- Premium business features remain unavailable until verification is complete.

If verification is unsuccessful, the customer will be notified by email with additional instructions.

5. ACCOUNT CREATION ISSUES

If a customer experiences a technical issue during account creation, the Support Team must collect:
- Customer email address.
- Error message or screenshot of the error.
- Date and time the issue occurred.
- Device and browser information, if available.

The issue must be escalated to the Technical Team within 2 hours of being reported.

Customers should receive an acknowledgment email confirming that the issue has been reported and is under investigation.
