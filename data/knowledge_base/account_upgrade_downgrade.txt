ACCOUNT UPGRADE AND DOWNGRADE POLICY

Effective Date: January 2024

1. UPGRADING AN ACCOUNT

Customers may upgrade their account plan at any time through the Account Settings page.

When an upgrade is completed:
- The upgrade takes effect immediately.
- New plan features become available immediately.
- The customer is charged the prorated difference between their current plan and the new plan for the remainder of the current billing period.

If payment for the upgrade cannot be processed, the upgrade request will not be completed until payment is successfully received.

2. DOWNGRADING AN ACCOUNT

Customers may downgrade their account plan at any time through the Account Settings page.

When a downgrade is requested:
- The downgrade becomes effective at the start of the next billing period.
- The customer retains access to all current plan features until the downgrade takes effect.
- No partial refunds are provided for the remaining portion of the current billing period.

Customers may cancel a pending downgrade request at any time before the next billing period begins.

3. FEATURE ACCESS AFTER DOWNGRADE

When a downgrade becomes effective:
- Access to features not included in the new plan is removed immediately.
- The customer must be informed of feature changes before confirming the downgrade.

If the customer has data that exceeds the limits of the lower plan:
- The data will remain accessible for 30 days.
- The customer may export or remove excess data during this period.
- After 30 days, excess data may become inaccessible until the account is upgraded or brought within plan limits.

4. UPGRADE AND DOWNGRADE ISSUES

If a customer experiences technical issues during an upgrade or downgrade:
- The issue must be escalated to the Technical Team immediately.

If a customer reports an incorrect charge:
- The Billing Team must be notified immediately.
- The charge must be reviewed within 1 business day.
- Any approved refund or billing adjustment must be processed within 3 business days.

Customers must provide:
- Account ID.
- Description of the issue.
- Date and time the issue occurred.
- Any billing receipts or error messages, if available.

5. ENTERPRISE PLAN CHANGES

Customers on Enterprise Plans cannot upgrade or downgrade through the self-service portal.

All Enterprise Plan changes must be handled by the Account Management Team.

Customers requesting an Enterprise Plan change should:
- Contact the Support Team.
- Provide their company name and account information.
- Request assistance from their assigned Account Manager.

Enterprise Plan change requests are typically reviewed within 2 business days.
