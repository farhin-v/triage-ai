SHIPPING AND DELIVERY POLICY

Effective Date: January 2024

1. DELIVERY TIMEFRAMES
Standard delivery takes 5 to 7 business days. Express delivery takes 2 to 3 business days. Overnight delivery takes 1 business day. Delivery timeframes begin from the date the order is dispatched not the date the order is placed. Customers receive a dispatch notification email with a tracking number when their order is shipped.

2. DELIVERY LOCATIONS
We deliver to all major cities and towns. Remote locations may require additional delivery time of 2 to 3 business days. Customers in remote locations are notified of the extended delivery time at checkout. International delivery is available to selected countries and takes 7 to 14 business days.

3. TRACKING AN ORDER
Customers can track their order using the tracking number provided in their dispatch notification email. The tracking page is updated every 24 hours. If a customer has not received a tracking number within 2 business days of placing their order they should contact support.

4. MISSED DELIVERIES
If a customer is not available at the time of delivery the courier will leave a delivery attempt card. The customer can rearrange delivery by contacting the courier directly using the details on the card. If the customer does not rearrange delivery within 5 business days the order will be returned to our warehouse and a refund will be issued.

5. DELIVERY CONFIRMATION
Customers receive a delivery confirmation email when their order is marked as delivered. If a customer receives a delivery confirmation but has not received their order they must contact support within 48 hours. Claims made after 48 hours may not be eligible for a replacement or refund.
